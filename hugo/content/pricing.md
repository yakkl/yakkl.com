- headline: What is Yakkl?
  textline: Yakkl is an open-source secure team collaboration application that runs on desktops and mobile devices. There are Free Personal Plans, Team Plans, and Enterprise Plans
  headline: Yakkl vs. Slack vs. Microsoft Teams?
  textline: Slack is a very good tool. In fact, I brought Slack Enterprise into a large public enterprise but ran into "channel sprawl" very fast. Channel Sprawl is how I describe the fact that you need to create a channel in Slack for everything. Meaning, if I have a team that may be working on a few different projects along with other teams then I will have to create a channel for 'Team 1 - Project 1' and then another channel for 'Team 1 - Project 2' and so on. There are many best practices according to Slack on how to handle this but you still have "Channel Sprawl".
- textline: Microsoft Teams is another good tool but it can be bulky and slow. It too has "Channel Sprawl" but not as bad as Slack.
